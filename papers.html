Paper Guidelines

Manuscripts must follow IEEE conference paper guidelines:

  • Manuscripts should be original, unpublished work.
  • Paper length: 4-6 pages including figures and references.
  • Use the IEEE conference template available at the IEEE website.
  • All submissions must be in PDF format.
  • Double-blind review: Do not include author names or affiliations in the manuscript.
  • References should follow IEEE style.
  • Figures and tables must be clear and properly captioned.
  • Submissions must be made through the authorized channels.
