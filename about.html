About AI Converge 2026

AI Converge 2026—Where Intelligence Meets Humanity! This international conference, hosted by the National Institute of Technology (NIT), Jamshedpur, India, is designed to bring together global minds: students, researchers, academicians, industry professionals, innovators, and social leaders—to explore the evolving impact of Artificial Intelligence on society, science, economy, and human life.

At its heart, AI Converge is a celebration of people and purpose. We believe AI is not just for coders—but for creators, farmers, doctors, and anyone ready to imagine a better world. The conference creates a space where deep technology meets human values, where innovation is not driven by profit, but by impact.

From cutting-edge research papers to hands-on innovation expos, from global keynote sessions to youth-led dialogues—this is where intelligence truly converges with humanity!
